About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Duke University, researching deep learning for medical image analysis. My work focuses on understanding and improving segmentation models using techniques like shape learning in CNNs, weak and noisy annotations, and model evaluation under real-world constraints.

While I value rigorous and theoretical research, I am particularly motivated by projects that intersect with practical challenges and can be applied in real-world, product-driven environments. I aim to work on solutions that address industry-relevant problems and contribute to the development of usable technologies.

Selected Research Projects

  • Failure Modes of SAM (2025)Technical: Designed and conducted experiments to analyze SAM’s failure in segmenting tree-like and low-contrast structures. Developed metrics to quantify "textural confusion" and co-authored a paper submitted to ICCV.
  • Slice Annotation Strategies (2025)Technical: Investigated cost-effective sparse annotation strategies for 3D medical image segmentation. Led experiment design and authored manuscript submitted to MIDL.
  • Segment Anything for Medical Images (2023)Applicational: Contributed to the evaluation of SAM compared to existing segmentation models. Developed an API to extend functionality and co-authored publication in Medical Image Analysis.
  • Weak Annotation Cost-Effectiveness Study (2023)Technical: Explored trade-offs between annotation quality and quantity using simulated weak annotations. Published first-author paper in Journal of Imaging Informatics in Medicine.
  • Placenta Accreta Detection (2024)Applicational: Collaborated with clinicians, facilitated dataset annotation, and deployed DeepLabV3+ with EMA-MA. Achieved high segmentation performance and published in UOG.
  • Shape Learning in CNNs (2024)Technical: Conducted empirical study on CNN reliance on shape vs. texture in semantic segmentation. Identified learning limitations and published first-author paper in Pattern Recognition.
  • UAV Path Planning Algorithm (2021)Technical: Developed novel path planning method using previously unused data sources. Achieved 2.5× improvement in efficiency. Published as first-author in conference.
  • Symbolic-AI for UAV Exploration (2021)Technical: Integrated symbolic reasoning into UAV navigation tasks. Co-authored publication in ICARA.
  • Deep RL for UAV Missions (2021)Applicational: Developed reinforcement learning approach for exploration in unknown environments. Published in ICUAS.
  • Wilderness Search and Rescue (2020)Applicational: Collaborated on RL-based rescue strategy design using simulation environments. Co-authored SSRR publication.
  • SVM-Based Program Identity Detection (2020–2021)Technical: Empirically evaluated SVM-based program fingerprinting. Diagnosed limitations in prior literature and completed undergraduate thesis.
